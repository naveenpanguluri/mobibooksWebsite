FINANCIAL ACCOUNTING & INVENTORY MANAGEMENT SOLUTION

Mobibooks solution includes a web portal, desktop and mobile applications. Solution works seamlessly due to centralized database on cloud. Each component of the solution addresses certain business needs. For instance, Top Management views dashboards and financial statements through web portal anywhere and at any time. Data is synchronized in real time, hence financial statements and dashboards are generated in real time. Accountants and or HR Managers administer user maintenance, company & location maintenance through web portal. Due to cloud and role based privileges, multiple users including auditors have access to the same version of the solution.

Accountants and or Store In-Charge set up accounts, post vouchers and generate reports through desktop application. Remote Cashiers or Field Executives use MobiField application to collect monies from the customer location. Systematically transactions are posted to daybooks within desktop application. Sales Agents capture customer and invoice data via MobiInvoice application. Invoice data is posted to applicable accounts within desktop application. Accounting staff reviews and prints invoices upon receipt of monies from customers.

MOBIBOOKS FEATURES

Desktop Financial Accounting

Manage chart of Accounts, Party Accounts, Vouchers and view financial reports.

Desktop Inventory Management

Handle inventory receipts, issues, transfers and adjustments balances. Both accounts are inventory are integrated.

Data Security & Access Controls

Your data is secured at every level right from transmission through user access. Used SSL tunnels to prevent leakages during data transmission.

Multi-Location support

Location specific user access permissions, Ledger access rights and reports.

Change History

Complete history of changes in mobibooks is recorded. Power users can retrieve or purge change history.

MobiDash App - Instant Access to Data

Using MobiDash app, Access your reports, ledgers, vouchers along with attachment and voice narrations from your smart phone.

MobiField App – Field Ops

Using MobiField app, Handle receipts, payments, deposits and transfers using mobile phone. Attach signatures, take pictures and voice narrations to the vouchers.

Ease of Invoicing

Using MobiInvoice app, Raise invoices from your smartphone / pad.

Open API Support

Easily integrate mobibooks with other third party applications such as payroll, dealer management systems, POS terminals, etc..
